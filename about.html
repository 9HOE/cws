About Casey's Web Solutions

My name is Casey Ty Lenio, founder of Casey’s Web Solutions. I specialize in creating professional websites for barbershops and small businesses.

I started this agency after seeing how many great local businesses either didn’t have a website or had one that didn’t reflect the quality of their work.

My mission is simple: build clean, modern websites that help businesses look professional, build trust, and attract more clients online — without overpriced contracts or confusing tech.

When you work with me, you communicate directly with the owner. No middlemen, no sales teams, and no hidden fees.

I focus on clear communication, fast turnaround times (typically 7–10 days), and honest pricing so business owners know exactly what they’re getting.
